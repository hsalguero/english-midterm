How Early American's Pursuit for God, Gold, and/or Glory Influenced Modern Society


Pre-Colonial

During this time europeans started to come to the New World and interact with the natives. Many of these Europeans came to the new world to pursue God, gold, and glory. In doing so many established settlements in newly discovered lands.

Colonial

During this time colonies started to take shape. These colonies allowed for new culture and ways of life to form. The differences between the colonies and their rulers in England caused tensions and increased the desire for independance.

Market Revolution

During this time their was rapid change; areas started to become more urban and city like. People started to look towards science for answers instead of God. Both of these changes increased peoples fear of the unknown.
